Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to start?

Start with a 3-day free trial. After that, continue for ₹299/month via Razorpay (fixed amount — no manual entry).

Do I need to know grammar first?

No. We teach Listen → Speak → Understand → Grammar — like a child learns. Grammar comes naturally after you can speak basic sentences.

Can I learn from Hindi?

Yes! English Buddy lets you speak in Hindi (or Bengali, Tamil, Telugu) and instantly get the English translation, meaning, and pronunciation guide.

How much time per day?

Just 5 minutes for the daily lesson. Optional speaking practice and games take 5–10 more minutes if you want.

Can I install it on my phone?

Yes! Tap "Install App" or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option. Works on Android and iPhone like a native app.
