How to Introduce Yourself in English (with Hindi)

Many Indians say Myself Rahul — grammatically incorrect. The natural form is My name is Rahul. Here are templates you can memorize and adapt.

Basic introduction

Hello. My name is Priya. I am from Patna. Nice to meet you.

नमस्ते। मेरा नाम प्रिया है। मैं पटना से हूँ। आपसे मिलकर अच्छा लगा।

College or school

Hi, I am Amit. I study Computer Science at Delhi University. I am in my second year. I enjoy coding and cricket.

Job interview (60 seconds)

Good morning. My name is Sneha Sharma. I am from Jaipur. I completed B.Com from Rajasthan University. I am passionate about accounts and I have interned at a CA firm for six months. I am a quick learner and I work well in teams. I am excited about this opportunity with your company.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • ❌ Myself [Name] → ✅ My name is [Name]
  • ❌ I am belonging to Delhi → ✅ I am from Delhi
  • ❌ My native is Hindi → ✅ My mother tongue is Hindi
