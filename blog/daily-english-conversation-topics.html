15 Daily English Conversation Topics

Pick one topic each day. Say at least five sentences aloud — about your real life. This builds fluency faster than silent reading.

  • 1. Weather: It is very hot today. I think it will rain in the evening.
  • 2. Food: I had idli for breakfast. My favourite dish is biryani.
  • 3. Traffic: There was heavy traffic on my way to office. The metro was crowded.
  • 4. Work or college: I have a deadline tomorrow. Our team meeting went well.
  • 5. Family: I called my mother yesterday. My brother is preparing for exams.
  • 6. Weekend plans: I will visit my cousin. We might watch a movie.
  • 7. Health: I started walking every morning. I should drink more water.
  • 8. Festival: Diwali is my favourite festival. We decorate the house with lights.
  • 9. Hometown: I am from Lucknow. I miss the food there.
  • 10. Shopping: I bought vegetables from the market. The price was reasonable.
  • 11. Technology: I use my phone for payments. I learned something new on YouTube.
  • 12. Goals: I want to speak English confidently. I will practice daily.
  • 13. Travel: I went to Goa last year. The beach was beautiful.
  • 14. Hobby: I like cooking and cricket. On Sundays I play with friends.
  • 15. News: I read the headlines today. Something interesting happened in sports.
