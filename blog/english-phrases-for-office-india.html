50 English Phrases for Office Life in India

Office English is about clarity and politeness, not difficult words. Learn these groups and use one group per day at work.

Email & chat (10 phrases)

Good morning, team. · Hope you are doing well. · Please find the attachment. · Could you review this by EOD? · I will send an update by tomorrow. · Thanks for your quick response. · Let me check and get back to you. · As discussed, please proceed. · Sorry for the late reply. · Have a great weekend.

Meetings (10 phrases)

Can we schedule a quick call? · I agree with your point. · Let me share my screen. · Can you repeat that, please? · I have a question. · Let us take this offline. · We are running out of time. · I will follow up on action items. · Thanks everyone for joining. · Let us wrap up for today.

With your manager (10 phrases)

I have completed the task. · I need your guidance on this. · I will update you by end of day. · Thank you for the feedback. · I understand the priority. · May I take leave on Friday? · I am facing a blocker. · Here is the status report. · I would like your approval. · I will improve on this point.

With colleagues (10 phrases)

Can you help me with this? · Sure, no problem. · I am on a deadline. · Let us collaborate on this. · Good job on the presentation. · I will cover for you. · Can we pair on this bug? · Lunch together? · See you in the standup. · Happy to help anytime.

Reporting problems (10 phrases)

There is an issue with the server. · The client called about a delay. · We need to escalate this. · I have informed the team. · The fix is in progress. · Testing is complete. · We deployed to production. · Rollback completed. · Root cause identified. · Preventive steps added.
